Welcome to Whoseagooddoggie

The site that aims to be a dog's second best friend.

Our mission

We want to provide dog owners with the tools to make their dogs happy and healthy. We believe that a healthy diet is the key to a happy dog.

How it works

This is a platform for dog owners to share their favorite recipes that can make their dogs wag their tails with joy.

  • 1. Sign up to the site
  • 2. Share your favorite doggy recipes
  • 3. Browse other recipes
  • 4. Rate and comment on recipes
  • 5. Upload photos of your dog enjoying his meals

Why a homemade diet?

Our site shares the benefits of a homemade diet with fresh ingredients and no artificial preservatives and other chemicals
Not forgeting that variety is the spice of even for dogs. Here's a list of the benefits of a healthy doggy diet:
      1. Improved digestion
      2. Healthier skin and coat
      3. Increased energy levels
      4. Better weight management
      5. Stronger immune system
      6. Better dental health
      7. Fewer allergies
      8. Fewer vet visits
      A longer lifespan of up to 2-3 years longer

We hope you enjoy the site and find some great recipes for your dog.
