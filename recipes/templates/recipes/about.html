About #Whoseagooddoggie

Good doggies should get the food they deserve

Our Mission

We want to provide dog owners with the tools to make their dogs happy and healthy. We believe that a healthy diet is the key to a happy dog.

Why go homemade?

There are loads of benefits of using fresh ingredients and no artificial preservatives or other chemicals. Not forgeting that variety is the spice of life, even for dogs. Here's a list of why it makes sense:

      1. Improved digestion
      2. Healthier skin and coat
      3. Increased energy levels
      4. Better weight management
      5. Stronger immune system
      6. Better dental health
      7. Fewer allergies
      8. Fewer vet visits
      A longer lifespan of up to 2-3 years longer
